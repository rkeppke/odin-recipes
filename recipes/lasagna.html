Lasagna recipe

How to make a Lasagna

Making a Lasagna can be time-consuming but it is well worth the wait! Follow below the detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • Sweet italian Sausage 1 pound
  • Lean ground Beef 3/4 pound
  • Minced Onion 1/2 cup
  • Garlic 2 crushed cloves
  • Crushed tomatoes 1 can(28 ounces)
  • Canned tomatoe sauce 2 (6.5 ounces)cans
  • Tomato paste 2 (6 ounces)cans
  • Water 1/2 cup
  • white sugar 2 tablespoons
  • Chopped fresh parsley 4 tablespoons
  • Dried Basil leaves 1 + 1/2 teaspoons
  • Salt 1 + 1/2 teaspoons
  • Fennel Seeds 1/2 teaspoons
  • Ground Black pepper 1/4 teaspoons
  • Lasagna Noodles 12
  • Ricotta Cheese 16 ounces
  • Egg 1
  • Sliced mozzarella cheese 3/4 pound
  • Grated parmesan cheese 3/4 cup

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat
    until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste,
    and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt,
    Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2
    hours, stirring occasionally.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling
    water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water. In a mixing
    bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2
    teaspoon salt.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese
    mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over
    mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, and top with
    remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either
    spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional
    25 minutes. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
